Refund policy

Last updated: pending review

Notice: this is a template for guidance only. EU law allows waiving the right of withdrawal for digital content if the customer agrees to immediate download. Review and adapt this text with your advisor before publishing the store. It does not constitute legal advice.

1. Downloadable digital content

Due to the nature of digital products, once the download has started or access to the content has been granted, the customer acknowledges losing the right of withdrawal under applicable consumer law.

2. When a refund is accepted

  • The file cannot be downloaded or is corrupted and cannot be fixed.
  • The product significantly does not match what is described on its page.
  • Duplicate purchase or incorrect charge.

3. Services and mentoring

Service/mentoring sessions can be rescheduled with reasonable notice. Cancellation and refund of sessions not yet delivered will be assessed according to the conditions stated on the product page.

4. How to request it

Request a refund from the contact page, providing the order number and the reason. As payment is handled by the payment provider (Merchant of Record), the refund is processed through that provider.

5. Timeframes

Valid requests are usually processed within 14 days; the credit may take a few additional days depending on the payment method.
